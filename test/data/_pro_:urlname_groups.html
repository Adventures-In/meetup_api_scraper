Search Pro Groups

GET /pro/:urlname/groups
  • json
Host: api.meetup.com
  • basic
api version 3

Name and statistics range search for the meetup groups belonging to Pro organization.

Try it in the console

Request Parameters

All parameters are optional. order and desc can take multiple items in a comma-delimited list.

active_within_days

Positive Integer: including only those groups that had event in the last specified days

average_age_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the average age of the members

average_age_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the average age of the members

category

List of Integers: the ids of the category of the group

chapter_urlnames

List of String: the urlnames of chapters that may belong to the organization

chapters

List of Integer: the chapter ids that may belong to the organization

country

String: Country

desc

List of Boolean: whether to sort ascending or decending on each attributes in order

excluded_chapters

List of Integer: the chapters to exclude from the result

founded_date_max

Milliseconds since epoch: Maximum range of the founded dates of the groups

founded_date_min

Milliseconds since epoch: Minumum range of the founded dates of the groups

inactive_within_days

Positive Integer: including only those groups that did not have event in the last specified days

last_event_max

Milliseconds since epoch: Maximum range of the date that the last meetup happened

last_event_min

Milliseconds since epoch: Minumum range of the date that the last meetup happened

lat

Float: Latitude

location

String: Raw location

lon

Float: Longitude

member_count_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the number of members

member_count_min

Positive Integer: Minimum range of the number of members

name

String: Name of the group looking for

next_event_max

Milliseconds since epoch: Maximum range of the date that the next meetup is scheduled

next_event_min

Milliseconds since epoch: Minumum range of the date that the next meetup is scheduled

offset

Positive Integer: the page offset

order

List of String: attributes to sort on

page

Positive Integer: the size of page window

past_events_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the number of the past events held

past_events_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the number of the past events held

past_rsvps_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the total number of past RSVPs

past_rsvps_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the total number of past RSVPs

pro_join_date_max

Milliseconds since epoch: Maximum range of the dates the groups joined Pro organization

pro_join_date_min

Milliseconds since epoch: Minumum range of the dates the groups joined Pro organization

query

String: raw query to search from group name, description, leadership member name, or city

radius

String: global, smart, or search radius in Float

repeat_rsvpers_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the average number of repeat rsvpers

repeat_rsvpers_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the average number of repeat rsvpers

rsvps_per_event_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the average number of RSVPs per event

rsvps_per_event_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the average number of RSVPs per event

topics

List of Integers: the ids of topic of the group

upcoming_events_max

Positive Integer: Maximum range of the number of the upcoming events

upcoming_events_min

Positive Integer: Minumum range of the number of the upcoming events

zip

String: Zip code

Ordering

average_age

By the average age of members

founded_date

By the founded date of the group

gender_female

By the proportion of people who identify themselves as female

gender_male

By the proportion of people who identify themselves as male

gender_other

By the proportion of people who identify themselves as gender other than female or male

gender_unknown

By the proportion of people whose gender info is unknown

last_event

By the date of the last event happened

member_count

By the number of members

name

By the name of the groups

next_event

By the date of the next event scheduled

past_events

By the number of past meetups held

past_rsvps

By the total number of past RSVPs

pro_join_date

By the date the group joined the Pro org

repeat_rsvpers

By the number of members RSVPing subsequent events

rsvps_per_event

By the average number of RSVPs per event
